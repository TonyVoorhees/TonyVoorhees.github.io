History

Having collaborated in a wide range of cross-functional teams over the years has taught me to seek the bigger picture. I recognize how vital my previous roles play in my future successes. It's what enables me to undertake more complex projects.

January 2007 - Present

Freelance Designer

Being a freelance designer is a discipline in itself. Constantly adapting to new tech stacks/apps, keeping an eye on the latest trends, building a sustainable network, all while looking for my next gig, are just a few of the imperative responsibilities. Creative exploration, problem-solving, and design execution are a few reasons why I love being a designer.

I regularly explore new opportunities so that I may further my education and experience. I'm a designer who enjoys his work. If you need creative direction or hands-on assistance with a project, please reach out.

Contact me

September 2017 - September 2019

Manager at Rosalind Bakery

Located in Pacifica, California, Rosalind Bakery is a 3400 sqft commercial baking facility that supplies grocery stores, restaurants, and farmers' markets with artisanal baked goods. For two years, I assisted in developing a recognizable brand around a line of naturally leavened products, including sourdough bread, baguettes, pretzels, and assorted pastries. Manager, baker, design maker, even general contractor were just a few of the hats I wore to help establish this startup.

October 2017 - October 2018

Product Designer at CardCruncher

Working remotely as a product designer, I helped this startup produce a web app MVP within four months to pitch to investors and validate a need in the fintech industry. I helped establish a UX roadmap based on an agile development cycle using personas, user flows, and user/business goals. I utilized the sketch app to design the UI kit, pattern library, and layouts. I also used InVision for prototyping and handoff to the dev teams. The initial investor review won the company's first round of pre-seed funding.

January 2017 - August 2017

UX/UI Designer at FSA Store

Contracted for eight months to enhance the UX at this startup, I was able to bring together various teams and modernize two e-commerce platforms. I helped establish a UX roadmap that provided a vision for stakeholders, identified user/business goals, timeframes for sprints, determined possible outcomes, and how to adapt the process. We successfully bootstrapped two responsive eCommerce platforms, created two UI kits with pattern libraries, and improve the user's experience across multiple devices. For user research, we utilized Crazyegg for heatmaps, Optimizely for A/B testing, and Google Analytics for more quantitative data.

July 2014 - January 2016

Creative Director at DraftKings

As a creative lead, I helped establish a team that supplied creative assets to cross-platform native apps, web apps, and various direct response marketing campaigns. I assisted in brand development, creating interactive style guides, and onboarding new talent.

Additionally, I worked alongside the creative team, where I assumed the role of art director in creating mobile concepts, designing promotions, and developing responsive landing pages.

Onward

Looking forward, I strive to inspire team collaboration across multiple disciplines to produce great user experiences. If you're looking for a mindful team player that can provide leadership at any phase of production, let's connect.
