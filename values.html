Values

In my career, I have come to recognize certain values that consistently hold true. I take pride in these values using them to help guide my decision making and methods.

Passion

I love being creative and expressing myself through my work. I find immense pleasure creating products and experiences that are entertaining with information that benefit people's lives.

Ambition

I rise above challenges I face each day and goals I’ve set for myself with enthusiasm. I have learned to persevere through tough times and embrace change to achieve excellence.

Empathy

I realize each person has unique experiences and perceptions of how things should work. I try to be conscious of other people’s views and try to relate before I criticize.

Humility

I take pride in my work and stand up for my principles, but I also have the confidence to admit when my idea is not the best option. I value feedback and utilize it as a resource for learning.

Collaboration

I welcome the opportunity to contribute and be a part of something greater than myself. I strive to be respectful, be mindful, and keep a positive attitude while working with others.

Curiosity

I live in a constant state of research and development, always trying to evolve in my knowledge and skills. I believe learning is fundamental to a great lifestyle.
