## Who we are

Studio Nord ApS ("we", "us") operates this website. If you have any questions about this policy, reach us at [hello@studionord.dk](mailto:hello@studionord.dk).

## What we collect

- **Information you give us.** When you submit the contact form, we receive your name, email, and the details of your message. We use these only to reply to your enquiry.
- **Usage data.** Like most sites, our hosting provider records basic, anonymous request logs (IP address, browser, pages visited) to keep the service secure and reliable.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

## Cookies

This site ships without tracking or marketing cookies. If you add analytics later, document it here and obtain consent where the law requires it.

## Third parties

We rely on a small number of processors to run the site — for example, our hosting provider and the service that delivers contact-form messages. Each only receives the data needed to perform its function.

## Your rights

Under the GDPR you can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, and you can object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, email us at the address above. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the studio or the law evolves. The "last updated" date above always reflects the current version.
