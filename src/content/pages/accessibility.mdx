We want everyone to be able to read our work and get in touch, regardless of how they browse. Accessibility is treated as part of the craft here, not an afterthought.

## Conformance

This site aims to meet **WCAG 2.1 Level AA**. We test with keyboard navigation, screen-reader spot checks, and automated audits as part of every release.

## What we've done

- **Semantic structure** — real headings, landmarks, and lists, so assistive technology can navigate the page.
- **Keyboard support** — every interactive element is reachable and operable without a mouse, with a visible focus state.
- **Colour contrast** — text meets AA contrast against its background in both the light and dark themes.
- **Reduced motion** — scroll reveals and transitions are disabled for visitors who prefer reduced motion.
- **Responsive text** — layouts reflow cleanly and stay usable when text is zoomed.

## Known limitations

No site is ever perfectly accessible. If you hit a barrier we've missed, we want to know about it.

## Feedback

Email [hello@studionord.dk](mailto:hello@studionord.dk) and we'll do our best to respond — and to fix the issue in a future update.
