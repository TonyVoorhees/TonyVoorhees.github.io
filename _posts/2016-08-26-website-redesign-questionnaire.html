This preliminary questionnaire is an essential part of the website building process. It will help better understand your company as well as provide the necessary information about your business, requirements, and expectations. The data collected will be used to prepare a more accurate proposal for a website redesign.

General

  1. What are the products/services offered by your company? (Please be specific)
  2. What are your company's core values?
  3. Who are your company’s top five competitors?
  4. Is your company on social media? If so, which websites or apps?
  5. What are some of the biggest challenges or concerns for your company online?

Technical

  1. Who is currently hosting your company's website?
  2. What is your company's website primary purpose?
  3. What actions would your company like its users to perform when visiting the website? (e.g. Information only, purchase a product, sign up for a service, etc.)
  4. What is your company's ideal device for viewing the website?
  5. Do you know how many people visit your company's website each day, week or month? (Any analytical data)
  6. Does your company have the ability to update the website now? (e.g. Blog, forms, video, graphs, etc.)

Marketing

  1. Who is your company’s target audience?
  2. What are the key reasons why the target user chooses your company’s products or services versus the competition?
  3. Who would your company like to reach with this website?
  4. Does your company have existing print collateral? (e.g. Business cards, letterhead, logo, marketing banners, etc.)
  5. Will your company need creative services in addition to a website? (e.g. Logo, copy, photos, artwork, etc.)

Redesign

  1. What are five goals your company hopes to achieve from this website redesign?
  2. Does your company plan to use content from the existing website? If so, which portions?
  3. Will your company want the ability to update the website? (e.g. Blog, video, graphs, etc.)
  4. What are some results your company would determine the website a success?
  5. Are there other considerations that could impact the website? (e.g. New product launch, marketing campaign, etc.)
  6. Has your company established a budget for this project? (If so, please specify. If not, estimated budget will help.)
