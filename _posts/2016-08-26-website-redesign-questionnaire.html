This preliminary questionnaire is an essential part of the website building process. It will help better understand your company as well as provide the necessary information about your business, requirements, and expectations. The data collected will be used to prepare a more accurate proposal for a website redesign.

General

  1. What are the services/products offered by your business? (Please be specific)
  2. What are your company's core values?
  3. Who is your company’s target audience?
  4. Who are your top five competitors?
  5. What are some of the biggest challenges or concerns for your company online?

Technical

  1. Who is currently hosting your company's website?
  2. What is the website’s primary purpose?
  3. What actions do you want your users to perform when visiting your website? (Examples: Information only, sign up for email, purchase a product, sign up for a service, etc)
  4. What is the ideal device to view the website?
  5. Do you know how many people visit your site each day, week or month? (Any analytical data)
  6. Does your company have the ability to update the site? (Blog, Forms, Video, Graphs, etc)

Marketing

  1. What are the key reasons why the target user chooses your company’s services or product versus the competition?
  2. Who would you like to reach with this website?
  3. Are you on social media? If so, which sites or apps?
  4. Do you have existing print collateral? (Examples: Business cards, letterhead, logo, marketing banners)
  5. Will you need creative services in addition to the website? (logo, copy, photos, artwork)

Redesign

  1. What are five goals this website hopes to achieve?
  2. Do you plan to use content from the existing site? If so, which portions?
  3. Will your company want the ability to update the website? (Blog, Forms, Video, Graphs, etc)
  4. What result would determine that the site is a success?
  5. Are there other considerations that could impact the website? (New product launch, marketing campaign)
  6. Have you established a budget for this project? (If so, please specify. If not, estimated budget will help.)
